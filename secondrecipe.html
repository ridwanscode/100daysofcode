Easy mac&cheese for when you want a cheesy feast

well are you looking for a cheesy dish to make?
everyone loves a mac&andcheese, and ill be teaching you today how to make a it,
quick and easy. A dish like this will leave everyone wanting seconds.
This dish will feed atleast 4 people.

    METHOD

  1. Oven temperature set at 200°C/180°F/gas 6.
    On a baking sheet, distribute the baguette pieces and season with salt and pepper.
    Drizzle with melted butter. Set aside after baking for 6 minutes till crisp.
  2. Drain the pasta after boiling it for 2 minutes shorter,
    than the package instructions. Melt the butter in a pot in the meanwhile.
    Cook for one minute after adding the garlic and English mustard powder,
    then whisk in the plain flour.
  3. Cook for 1 min more, then gradually whisk in the milk until you have a lump-free sauce.
    Simmer for 5 mins, whisking all the time until thickened.
    Take off the heat, then stir in the cheddar and half the parmesan.
  4. The cheese sauce should be combined with some seasoning and pasta.
    Pour into four separate servings or a large ovenproof dish.
  5. Scatter over the bread and the remaining parmesan,
    then bake for 20 mins until crisp and golden.
    Can be frozen before baking defrost thoroughly before cooking.

    ingridients

  • 50g baguette, cut into small chunks
  • 2 tbsp butter, plus 1 tbsp melted
  • 350g spiral or other short pasta
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp English mustard powder
  • 3 tbsp plain flour
  • 500ml whole milk
  • 250g vegetarian mature cheddar, grated
  • 50g parmesan (or vegetarian alternative), grated
